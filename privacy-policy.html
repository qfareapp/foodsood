FoodSood and NeighbourBites Chef

Privacy Policy and Data Safety Summary

This page applies to both the buyer app and the chef app. It explains what data we collect, why we use it, how we protect it, and how users can request deletion or support.

Applies to:
FoodSood buyer app and NeighbourBites Chef app
Effective date:
May 6, 2026
Support contact:
support@foodsood.com

1. What We Collect

Depending on whether you use the buyer app or chef app, we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information such as name, phone number, email address, user role, and login credentials.
  • Profile information such as bio, avatar, cover image, kitchen images, dish details, ratings, and reviews.
  • Location information such as city, address text, and foreground GPS coordinates when you choose to provide them.
  • Order and marketplace information such as requests, quotes, dishes, offers, delivery mode, order status, and payment references.
  • Device and app information such as secure login tokens and push notification tokens.
  • User-generated content such as profile text, uploaded images, reviews, and moderation reports.

2. Why We Use Data

  • To create and manage buyer and chef accounts.
  • To show nearby chefs, delivery areas, and order-relevant locations.
  • To enable food requests, quotes, offers, direct dish sales, and order fulfillment.
  • To show reviews, ratings, profile content, and live dish listings.
  • To send important push notifications, such as quote updates and order progress.
  • To enforce safety controls, including user blocking, reporting, moderation, and abuse review.
  • To improve reliability, fraud prevention, and operational support.

3. Permissions Used In The Apps

  • Location: used to help set service areas, buyer addresses, and nearby chef discovery.
  • Photos / media library: used when a user chooses to upload profile, kitchen, or food images.
  • Camera: used in the chef app when a chef chooses to take profile or kitchen photos.
  • Notifications: used in the chef app for order and marketplace alerts.
We do not use background location in the current apps. Location access is requested only while the app is in use.

4. How We Share Data

  • Buyer and chef profile information may be shown to other users when needed for order discovery and fulfillment.
  • Reviews, ratings, and chef profile content may be visible to other users inside the app.
  • We may use service providers for infrastructure, image hosting, notifications, and transactional support.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may disclose data where required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or platform security.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and order data only as long as necessary for app operation, fraud prevention, support, dispute handling, and legal obligations. Some records may be kept for a limited period after account closure when required for these purposes.

6. Account Deletion and Data Requests

You can request account deletion from inside the app when that feature is available, or by contacting us through the web or support channels listed on this page. When we delete an account, we also delete or anonymize the associated user data unless we must retain limited records for legal, fraud-prevention, or security reasons.

Deletion requests and privacy questions can be sent to support@foodsood.com.

7. User-Generated Content and Safety

Both apps support user-generated content, including profile text, images, dish descriptions, and reviews. We provide in-app reporting, blocking, and admin moderation tools to help review objectionable content and account abuse.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data, including authenticated access, token-based sessions, and restricted admin access. No system can guarantee absolute security, so users should also protect their own credentials and devices.

10. Contact

For privacy, deletion, moderation, or support issues, contact support@foodsood.com.
