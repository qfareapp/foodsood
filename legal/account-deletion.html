Account Deletion Request

This page is the public account-deletion path for both the buyer app and the chef app. You can also start the same request from inside the app profile section.

Submitting this request may deactivate your account before full deletion processing is completed. Limited records may be retained where required for fraud prevention, payment disputes, safety review, or legal compliance.

How deletion works

  • In-app authenticated requests can immediately deactivate the account.
  • Public web requests are reviewed using the contact details you provide below.
  • Associated personal data is deleted or anonymized unless limited retention is required for security, legal, or dispute reasons.
