FoodSood and NeighbourBites Chef

Privacy Policy and Data Safety Summary

This page applies to both the buyer app and the chef app. It explains what data we collect, why we use it, how we protect it, and how users can request deletion or support.

Applies to:
FoodSood buyer app and NeighbourBites Chef app
Effective date:
May 6, 2026
Support contact:
support@foodsood.com

What We Collect

  • Account information such as name, phone number, email address, role, and login credentials.
  • Profile information such as bio, avatar, cover image, kitchen images, dish details, ratings, and reviews.
  • Location information such as city, address text, and foreground GPS coordinates when you choose to provide them.
  • Order and marketplace information such as requests, quotes, dishes, offers, delivery mode, order status, and payment references.
  • Device and app information such as secure login tokens and push notification tokens.
  • User-generated content such as profile text, uploaded images, reviews, and moderation reports.

Why We Use Data

  • To create and manage buyer and chef accounts.
  • To show nearby chefs, delivery areas, and order-relevant locations.
  • To enable food requests, quotes, offers, direct dish sales, and order fulfillment.
  • To show reviews, ratings, profile content, and live dish listings.
  • To send important push notifications, such as quote updates and order progress.
  • To enforce safety controls, including user blocking, reporting, moderation, and abuse review.
We do not use background location in the current apps. Location access is requested only while the app is in use.

Data Safety Summary

  • We collect account details, user-generated content, location shared in-app, uploaded images, and order records.
  • We use this data for account management, marketplace operation, fulfillment, moderation, and support.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Privacy and deletion requests can be sent to support@foodsood.com.
